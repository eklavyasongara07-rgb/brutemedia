Every client you close comes through one of three engines. Master all three simultaneously — never rely on just one.

08 · THE SALES SYSTEM

The 10-Minute Close Call

You have 10 minutes. That's it. This is not a pitch meeting — it's a qualification and close. Follow this script exactly.

MINUTE YOUR SCRIPT PURPOSE
0-2 "Quick question before we get into it — what's your biggest goal for your Instagram in the next 90 days?" Qualify + get them talking
2-4 "And what's stopping you from getting there right now?" Identify the pain
4-6 "Here's what we do: [4 reels/week, scripted + edited]. Athletes we work with typically see [result] in 30 days." Present the solution
6-8 "Investment is $1,200/month, 30-day minimum. Does that work for your budget?" Price anchor + qualify
8-10 "Great — I'll send the agreement now. You can sign today and we start Monday." Close + urgency

Objection Handling

Objection 1: "It's too expensive"

RESPONSE
"I get that. Let me reframe it for you. $1,200/month works out to $40/day. If one sponsor deal, one product line sale, or one coaching client comes from your improved Instagram this month, you've already paid for it. The athletes I work with aren't spending money — they're investing in their brand. And their brand IS their income at the Pro level. Does $40/day feel different?"

Objection 2: "I need to think about it"

RESPONSE
"Totally fair. What specifically do you need to think about? Is it the price, the results, or whether this is the right time? [Listen to answer] Got it. Here's what I'd suggest: let's do a 30-day trial. You see results or you don't. If you don't, you cancel and owe nothing extra. If you do — which you will — we continue. Does that remove the risk for you?"

Objection 3: "I'll do it myself"

RESPONSE
"100% you could. The question is — what's your time worth? If you're spending 10-15 hours a week scripting, filming, editing, and analysing content, that's time not spent training, dieting, or competing. And most athletes who try to DIY it burn out in 60 days. What's your hourly rate if you were coaching or doing PTs? [They answer] So 15 hours a week = [$X]. We cost $1,200/month. You do the math."

12 · CONTINGENCY PLAYBOOK

Pivot Scenarios

Scenario A: Low DM Reply Rate (<10%)

Problem: Your opening message isn't resonating. Fix this before Day 14 or Phase 1 fails.
Rewrite your cold DM opener — make it more specific to their recent show placement
Test 3 different openers simultaneously (A/B test your messages)
Switch from text DMs to voice note as first touch (higher open rate)
Check that you're targeting Placements 6th-16th — 1st-3rd place athletes get too many offers

Scenario B: High Calls, Low Close Rate (<20%)

Problem: The leads are interested but you're losing them on the call. Your pitch or pricing is the issue.
Record your next 5 calls and listen back — find where people disengage
Test a lower entry price: "30-day trial at $800" instead of $1,200
Send a "risk reversal" offer: "Pay nothing until you see 30 days of results"
Address the objection earlier in the call, not after they've checked out

Scenario C: Client Churn (>2 clients leaving in 30 days)

Problem: Delivery isn't matching the promise. Revenue is being lost as fast as it's gained.
Call every churned client personally and ask what went wrong — no excuses, just listen
Add a 30-day check-in call to all active client contracts immediately
Review your editor quality — are the reels good enough to justify $1,200/month?
Under-promise and over-deliver: reduce reel commitments, add bonuses

Scenario D: Stuck at 6-7 Clients (Can't Break Through)

This is a mindset and capacity issue, not a market issue. The clients are there. You're hitting a ceiling in your own execution.
Audit your time: are you spending more than 2 hours/day on delivery work? Hand more to editors.
Increase your DM volume back to 20+/day — if you're at 6 clients, you've slowed outreach
Run a "case study push" week: post daily client results to create FOMO
Ask your 6-7 clients directly for referrals with a strong incentive

Recovery Plan: If Day 60 = Less Than 6 Clients

Emergency Protocol: You're behind. Here's how you recover in 30 days.
  1. Audit everything: Pull your Google Sheet data. Where exactly is the drop-off — DMs, calls, or closes?
  2. Fix the bottleneck only: Don't change everything. Find the one broken step and fix it this week.
  3. Run a "Founding Client" sprint: Offer $800/month locked for life for the next 3 clients who sign in the next 7 days. Create real urgency.
  4. Triple your outreach: 40-50 DMs per day for 2 weeks. Volume solves most problems at this stage.
  5. Post daily: 7 reels in 7 days on your personal page documenting the recovery. Transparency creates trust and leads.
